Signature.io is an API Platform for signing documents. Use Signature.io to integrate e-signing into your web or mobile application in minutes instead of months.



Get Started

Using Signtaure.io is simple. You begin by creating your account.

curl -X POST https://www.signature.io/api/v0/people.json \ 
-d "first_name=YOUR_FIRST_NAME" \ 
-d "email=YOUR_EMAIL" \ 
-d "password=YOUR_PASSWORD"

On a success response, you will receive your secret_api_key and your public_api_key. Use your secret key in the next step.

curl -X POST https://www.signature.io/api/v0/documents.json \ 
-u YOUR_SECRET_KEY: \ 
-d "url=https://www.signature.io/pdfs/sign-below.pdf"

On a success response, you will receive a share_url. Visit or share the share_url with the person you need to sign the document.

That's it. You are done. You will be notified by email when the document is signed.
